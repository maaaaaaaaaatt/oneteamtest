WELCOME TO GROUP LICENSING 101

Group licensing is when athletes come together and leverage their collective name, image and likeness (NIL) value to get deals done with brands. It offers an efficient and accessible way for athletes to obtain and share NIL revenue.

100% of the players in the NFL, MLB, WNBA, NBA and MLS participate in those respective sport group licensing programs.


This is how products like video games, trading cards and name and number jerseys get to market.


Group NIL rights are represented by a third party, like OneTeam, that negotiates on behalf of the athletes to maximize what a brand pays to include their NIL on retail products.
